Problem

The financial sector today remains one of the fields that requires the most manual processing. Banks, companies, investors, and auditing organizations are forced to read financial reports manually, verify numbers, and analyze thousands of pages every day. This process is not only slow but also highly prone to human error.
  • •More than 55% of financial analysts' time is spent collecting and cleaning data — not analyzing it.(Gartner, Forrester)
  • •96% of auditors and analysts report manual input errors when working with Excel or PDF documents.(Harvard, IBM, ESRIG)
  • •Large corporations handle on average 4,500+ pages of financial reporting annually — reading all of this manually takes approximately 6–14 days.(SEC, PwC, Deloitte)
  • •Incorrect or inaccurate financial calculations cost businesses 3–8% financial loss every year.(ACFE, PwC, Gartner)
  • •An average company in Uzbekistan works with 25–40 financial documents (PDF/Excel/CSV) every month, which is a heavy operational burden even for small businesses.(DSQ, State Statistics, bank & internal reporting)
    • This leads to several real issues::
    • 1Delays in making financial decisions
    • 2Risk of incorrect calculations
    • 3Excessive manual work in banking and fintech processes
    • 4Low efficiency in customer service
    • 5Limited access to financial analysis for small businesses
    The problem is urgent and widespread.

    Solution

    The platform developed by the MAAB talents team is an AI-powered system for automatic reading and analysis of financial statements.
    Our solution provides:
    • •Automatic extraction of data from PDF/Excel/CSV documents
    • •Accurate question-answer capabilities using an LLM fine-tuned on the FinQA dataset
    • •AI-driven analysis, insights, trends, and forecasts based on report data
    • •Automatic calculation of key metrics such as revenue growth, margin, EBITDA, debt-to-equity ratio, and more
    • •A web interface for uploading documents, receiving summaries, interacting with a chatbot, and exporting results
    This solution:
    • ✓Speeds up the work of banking and corporate analysts
    • ✓Reduces errors
    • ✓Elevates decision-making to a data-driven level
    • ✓Makes professional financial analysis affordable and accessible for small businesses
